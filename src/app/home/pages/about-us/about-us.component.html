About Us

MAKING A CHANGE


We're making a change one college student at a time. We're driven to change the talent pipeline by ensuring scholars of color and minorities have a chance to compete, no matter their zip code or the college they attend.

While working in Corporate America, College Career Connections co-founder Christopher Williams identified the disparity of young minorities in sustainable career positions. Seeking a way to close the gap and provide a platform for minorities to have the opportunity to compete and be identified, he launched the company Quazi. Quazi did several focus groups and surveys to determine the best opportunity and model.

College Career Connection was created to ensure that all minority college students have equal access to sustainable careers. Since our 2016 founding in Columbia, SC, our mission has become a reality. Within determination and persistence, College Career Connection will become the leading model for minorities' career communities in the US.


Mission Statement

Our mission is to develop a platform that provides sustainable career opportunities for minorities. We want them to build a career, not a job.


What We Do

We understand the needs of companies when it comes to hiring diverse talent. Therefore, we decided to create a software platform that allows companies to recruit, screen, and hire great people.
Incorporating technology with great people creates a platform that levels the playing field for minorities.

Why College Career Connections

Why choose College Career Connections? We are the only hiring platform dedicated to creating opportunities for minorities. We focus on efforts and energy on ensuring early career seekers have a dedicated place to craft their skill sets, increasing their marketability.


What's Make College Career Connections Different


  • What We Believe
    We believe anyone can find a job, but it takes a special person to find a career. College Career Connections positions its platform to help that special person.
  • Solutions
    Our platform provides a solution to assist with the company's diversity and inclusion initiative while hiring great minority talent.
  • Partnerships
    College Career Connections is changing the hiring landscape one career opportunity at a time. We are partnering with an organization to change the landscape, not just seeing them as a vendor.
